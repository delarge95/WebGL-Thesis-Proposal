# JOB SEARCH STRATEGY: "THE HUNT"
> **Target:** Junior Technical Artist / QA Automation (Gaming)
> **Location:** Remote / LATAM Nearshore
> **Start Date:** March 9, 2026

---

## 1. THE STRATEGY: "THE TROJAN HORSE"

**Reality:** Junior Technical Artist roles are rare.
**Tactic:** Apply for **QA Automation** or **Tools Developer** roles to enter the studio, then pivot internally using your Art skills as leverage.

**Why it works:**
- You have Engineering background (QA requires Python/C#).
- You have Art background (QA usually lacks this).
- You understand the pipeline better than a standard SDET.

### Primary Targets (The "Blue Ocean")
These are outsourcing studios that *always* need technical bodies.

| Company | Focus | Role to Apply |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| **Kokku (Recife/Remote)** | Co-Dev AAA | Junior Tech Artist / QA Tech |
| **1518 Studios (PTW)** | Art/QA | QA Automation |
| **Magic Media** | Mobile/Porting | Unity Developer (Optimization) |
| **Pingle Studio** | Porting | Optimization Engineer |
| **CGBot (Monterrey/Remote)** | Tech Art | Tools Programmer |

### Secondary Targets (The "Reach")
- **Globant Gaming:** Hard to enter as Junior, but massive hiring volume.
- **Keywords Studios:** Often has "FQA" (Functional QA) - avoid unless it's LQA/Technical.

## 3. THE DAILY ROUTINE (Starting March 09)

**Discipline > Motivation.** Treat the search as a 9-5 job.

| Time | Activity | Detail |
|------|----------|--------|
| **08:00 - 09:30** | **Cold Applications** | Apply to 3-5 jobs. Use "Easy Apply" but customize the resume summary. |

| **16:00 - 17:00** | **Community** | Active participation in Discord (Ask Gamedev, etc.). Help someone else. |

## 4. NETWORKING SCRIPT (The "Soft Entry")

**Do NOT ask for a job.** Ask for *advice*.

**Message to a Lead Tech Artist:**
> "Hi [Name], I'm a Technical Artist from Colombia specializing in Unity URP optimization. I've been following [Studio]'s work on [Game].
>
> I'm currently pivoting from Engineering to Tech Art and built a mobile optimization case study (profiling a high-poly drone for WebGL).
>
> Could I send you the breakdown to get your critique on my profiling methodology? I want to ensure my standards match what [Studio] looks for. No pressure at all."

**Why this works:** It strokes their ego ("I want your critique") and shows work ("profiling methodology") rather than begging.

## 5. PANIC PROTOCOL (May 01, 2026)

**Trigger:** It is May 1st and you have 0 offers. Runway ends in 30 days.

1.  **Pivot to BPO (Temporary):** Apply to *bilingual* Call Centers with chat support focus (allows some mental energy preservation).
2.  **Pivot to Standard Web Dev:** Apply for Junior React/Frontend jobs (your portfolio site proves you know web).
3.  **Freelance Upwork:** "Unity Bug Fixing" gigs ($15-20/hr). Low pay, but survival money.
4.  **CONTINUE APPLYING:** Do not stop the Tech Art hunt while working the survival job.
